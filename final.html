Final Project

Below you will find my game that I created along with my partner Zach Smith called Bitmoji Runner. Inspired by the Frogger assignment we did earlier in the semester, we decided to make a side-scroller game based off the hidden "Dinosaur Game" in Google Chrome where you jump over cacti in a desert. To play Bitmoji Runner, choose a character and the game will immediately begin. You start with 10 lives. Press the space bar to jump and avoid the cacti for as long as you can! Once you run out of lives, a death screen will pop up indicating as such. You can click Restart to play again.
